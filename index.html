INTRODUCTION:
A sleuthing, supernaturally infused mystery charting Wednesday Addams' time as a student at Nevermore Academy. Following Wednesday's attempts to master her emerging psychic ability, thwart a monstrous killing spree that has terrorized the local town, and solve the supernatural mystery that embroiled her parents 25 years ago — all while navigating her new and very tangled relationships.


CHARACTERS:

Wednesday Addams is a fictional character from The Addams Family dark comedy franchise. Like other characters in her family, she’s known for her spooky personality and Gothic appearance. Wednesday Addams, the daughter of the family, was likely named after a nursery rhyme containing the line “Wednesday’s child is full of woe.” The poem, which assigns personalities to children based on the day they were born, dates back to at least 1838. The television character was, like the rest of her family, unconventionally morbid and cold, with a penchant for chopping off dolls’ heads.

Morticia Addams is the wife of Gomez Addams and mother of Wednesday, Pugsley and Pubert Addams. The character originated in the Charles Addams cartoons for The New Yorker magazine in the 1930s. In the cartoons, none of the family members had names.

Gomez Addams is an athletic, acrobatic, and eccentric multi-billionaire. Though an extremely successful businessman, having acquired much of his wealth through inheritance and investments, he has little regard for money and will casually spend thousands of dollars on any whimsical endeavor.

Pugsley Addams is the son of Gomez Addams and Morticia Addams in the various cartoon, television, book, and film incarnations of The Addams Family. He is also the younger brother of Wednesday Addams.

Enid Sinclair is Wednesday’s bubbly and colorful roommate at Nevermore Academy. She’s a werewolf who is ostracized in her family for not being able to “wolf out.” While the two begin their cohabitation at odds, Enid and Wednesday form an unexpected friendship throughout the semester.

Xavier Thorpe is a mysterious student with the power to bring his drawings to life at Nevermore Academy, who also takes a liking to Jenna Ortega's Wednesday Addams. Xavier is an artist tortured by the legacy of his father Vincent Thorpe, a renowned psychic who has little time for his son.

Tyler Galpin is the normie son of Jericho’s Sheriff Galpin. The teen character works at the local coffee shop, Weathervane Cafe, where he forms a close connection to Wednesday Addams.

Principal Larissa Weems is the principal at Nevermore Academy who dislikes the Addams family. Wednesday’s character was the roommate of Morticia Addams when the two were students at Nevermore, although she still holds a grudge against Wednesday’s mother 30 years later.

Marilyn Thornhill is the dorm mother at Ophelia Hall, the residence of Wednesday and Enid. Miss Thornhill is the first and only normie member of Nevermore Academy’s staff in Wednesday, where she also teaches botany.

Sheriff Donovan Galpin is the normie sheriff in Jericho, who is also Tyler’s father. Sheriff Galpin is quickly at odds with Wednesday Addams, who has found herself in the middle of a supernatural murder investigation while spending an increasing amount of time with his son.
